Ayurveda offers a wide range of treatments including herbal medicine, dietary changes, yoga, meditation, detoxification, massage therapies like Abhyanga, Panchakarma cleansing, and lifestyle modifications.
Ayurveda views each person as unique, categorizing individuals into body types or doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha). Treatments are personalized, considering one's dosha, imbalances, and overall constitution.
Ayurveda can address various conditions including stress, digestive issues, chronic diseases, skin problems, hormonal imbalances, and promoting general well-being.
When practiced by trained professionals, Ayurvedic treatments are generally safe. However, it's essential to consult qualified practitioners and inform them about any existing health conditions or ongoing treatments.
